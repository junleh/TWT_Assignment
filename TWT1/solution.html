Solutions to Air Pollution

Promote Clean Energy

Encourage the use of renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power over fossil fuels. Subsidies and incentives can make these alternatives more attractive.

Improve Public Transportation

Invest in efficient and well-maintained public transport systems to reduce reliance on individual vehicles, which are major contributors to urban air pollution.

Regulate Industrial Emissions

Enforce strict regulations on industries to control emissions of pollutants like sulfur dioxide (SO2), nitrogen oxides (NOx), and particulate matter. Regular monitoring and penalties for non-compliance are essential.

Address Agricultural Practices

Promote sustainable agricultural techniques to minimize burning of crop residues and use of chemical fertilizers, which release harmful pollutants.

Urban Planning and Green Spaces

Plan cities with green belts, parks, and efficient waste management systems to reduce the heat island effect and improve air quality.
